Celebrate Your Special Day at Sparrow in Kirkland

 Host your elopement, micro-wedding, or vow renewal in Sparrow’s elegant heated sunroom, an intimate and cozy venue perfect for up to 20 guests. Located in Kirkland’s vibrant Juanita neighborhood, Sparrow offers a refined yet inviting space that provides the ideal backdrop for your celebration.

Our fully comprehensive package includes:

Flowers, decorations, ceremony coordination, live music, 3-course meal, appetizers and a great atmosphere..

At Sparrow, we take care of every detail, so you can focus on what matters most—celebrating your love. Our team is dedicated to creating a seamless and unforgettable experience for you and your guests.

Pricing information

Our exclusive package is available for $5,000 for the first 10 guests. This includes flowers, decorations, ceremony coordination, live music, a three-course meal, and welcome charcuterie board. Beverages and cocktails are available at an additional cost.

For events with more than 10 guests, an additional $200 per guest will apply, with a maximum capacity of 20 guests total.

Let Sparrow be the host of your intimate and memorable celebration—where elegance, exceptional service, and a warm atmosphere come together to create the perfect day.
